The Best Movies According to Connie

My top 3 movies of all-time

Howl's Moving Castle

I love the story line, music and pictures. Everytime I rewatch this movie, it feels a getaway from reality and living Sophie's life with her in Europe.

Whisper of the Heart

Tokyo city looks so pretty pictured by this director. All the details about charaters' daily lives brought back my memory grown up in the 90's. I love the theme that boys and girls are chasing their dreams in real life not as a fantacy.

The Harry Potter Films

As a fan of the book series, watching these movies definitely make me feel that magic is real!!!
